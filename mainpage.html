Bio

Juliet Wynn is a professional makeup artist specializing in Bridal, red carpet, editorial, and fasion makeup aristry. Juliet was educated in the art of makeup by Von Lee International School of Aesthetics in Baltimore Maryland. She primarily services the DC, Maryland and Virginia areas but she is willing to travel anywhere to meet her clients' needs.
